About SnapEat

Our Story & Mission

SnapEat started in 2024 with a simple idea: healthy food should be easy and accessible to everyone. Our team of nutritionists and chefs work together to create personalized meal plans that fit your lifestyle and taste preferences. From day one we've focused on sustainability, local sourcing, and using technology to make meal planning effortless.

Whether you're a busy professional, a parent juggling tasks, or someone simply looking to eat better, SnapEat is here to make healthy eating convenient and enjoyable. Join us on our journey to revolutionize the way people eat.
